About us

Qwery Academy is an organisation that is concerned with raising proper programmers, who understand the Nitti gritty of what it takes to build great platforms that work properly.

At Qwery Academy, we are aware of the challenges faced amongst individuals so we are set to train individuals to become excellent programmers with business acumen. Various courses would be taught that would help students equip themselves in the coding world; you learn to design various softwares such as websites, web applications and mobile applications; Learning various programming languages as you go along in the various courses.


There are so many programming languages; HTML, JAVASCRIPT, PYTHON, JAVA just to name a few and at Qwery Academy you would learn a lot about these languages.

Our goal is to build platforms not just to learn coding languages for knowledge sake but also to help the student discover which language he/she is most comfortable with, how he/she should approach difficulties, which way works best.

This academy is different, no cramming or learning just to “seem” smart, we’ll query everything. The questions WHY, HOW, WHEN AND WHERE?? Are our watchwords. You always have to see the big picture.

The team of developers who would teach are people who are able to break down concepts to basic levels and would even help you think the right way as a developer; how do you source for information as a developer if you are stuck on a project. We are raising developers who can also think on their own, because in real life, employers or people in business are attracted to problem solvers and not liabilities.
